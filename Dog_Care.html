Dog Care

Feeding

  • Puppies eight to 12 weeks old need four meals a day.
  • Feed puppies three to six months old three meals a day.
  • Feed puppies six months to one year two meals a day.
  • When your dog reaches his first birthday, one meal a day is usually enough.
  • For some dogs, including larger canines or those prone to bloat, it's better to feed two smaller meals.
List from ASPCA

Exercise

Every dog needs to exercise not just to burn calories, but it stimulates their minds, and it allows them to stay healthy. Depending on the dog breed there's a different amount of need for exercise. So, make sure to have supervised fun and games with your pets and make sure it satisfies his instinctual urges.

Housing

Your pet needs a warm quiet place to rest. Usually, dogs like a training crate or a dog bed with a clean blanket or pillow for questioning. Make sure to clean the bed off then because dogs spend a lot of time outdoors where they get dirty. And in your house make sure the dog has access to shade and cool water.
